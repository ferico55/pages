4 — ETH-specific risks to know before swapping
Bitcoin Season timing lag
BTC dominance at 58.5% = Bitcoin Season. ETH tends to lag BTC in early recovery, then catches up and outperforms. Don't be surprised if BTC gains first and ETH takes a few extra weeks to move.
Glamsterdam upgrade delay risk
Ethereum has a history of pushing upgrade timelines. If Glamsterdam slips into Q4 2026, one of ETH's key mid-term catalysts disappears temporarily. Not fatal — but don't count on it as a certainty.
Correlated downside
BTC and ETH are ~85–90% correlated in a broad market crash. BTC+ETH is an upside diversification strategy, not a crash hedge. If the whole market sells off, both go down together.
ETH outperforms in every recovery cycle
In every post-bear recovery since 2019, ETH has ultimately outperformed BTC from the bottom. Entering at Extreme Fear (index: 11) has historically been one of the best ETH entry points across multiple cycles.

Step 4 — Record both entry prices. Your BTC and ETH cost bases are your reference for every future decision.
